MARINE FUEL OIL

Download

Marine Fuel Oil (MFO) or High Sulfur Fuel Oil (HSFO) is low speed diesel engine fuel < 300 rpm which has a viscosity of up to max. 180 cSt and high Sulfur content up to max. 3.5% v/v. MFO180 is used in internal combustion engines and external combustion engines such as in industry and power plants.
