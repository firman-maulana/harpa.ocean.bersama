Established in 2010, PT. Harpa Ocean Bersama (HOB) is a trusted bunker service provider known for its reliability, integrity, and transparency. We support Indonesia’s maritime industry by delivering dependable fuel solutions across key sea routes and remote regions nationwide.
As the official agent and distributor appointed by AKR, HOB specializes in supplying high-quality AKR High Speed Diesel (HSD) and Marine Fuel Oil (MFO), ensuring efficient and reliable energy for vessels across the archipelago.
  • Reliable & Transparent Operations
  • Official AKR Agent & Distributor
  • High-Quality HSD & MFO Supply

Delivery Dreams

Committed to Leading Indonesia’s Bunkering Industry

HOB has undergone significant evolution since its establishment in 2010, starting with just one tanker and growing to operate a fleet of eight fuel tankers. Additionally, HOB has expanded its ownership to include eight vessels, providing comprehensive coverage of Indonesian waters. Our relentless commitment is to further expand our fleet, with the ultimate goal of becoming the leading fuel bunker agent in Indonesian waters.

Our commitment to quality and safety sets us apart in the industry. At HOB Tankers, we take great pride in supplying the fuel that drives international businesses and industries exploring the vast Indonesian waters. This not only fuels the economy of Indonesia but also benefits the origin countries of the ships we serve.
