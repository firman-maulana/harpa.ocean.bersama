Steady Growth

PT. HARPA OCEAN BERSAMA was established in Surabaya based on the TOSIN notary deed, SH, deed No. 3 on March 4, 2010. This company is involved in the distribution of fuel oil, both on land and at sea.
With its headquarters in Surabaya, East Java, Indonesia, the company has emerged as one of Indonesia's leading fuel sea transportation providers, offering comprehensive Bunker Service Agency and Operator solutions.

Established in 2010, PT. Harpa Ocean Bersama (HOB) is a trusted and transparent company specializing in Bunker Service Agency and Bunker Operations across Indonesia.

Appointed by AKR Corporindo as an official agent and distributor, HOB provides high-quality High-Speed Diesel (HSD) and Marine Fuel Oil (MFO) nationwide under cooperation agreement No. 288-B/C-CL/2022 dated September 3, 2022.
